Ep 348: The Man, The Fighter and The Kid

Jun 16, 2015 · 10 min · Ten Minute Podcast

Will recently paid a visit to the podcast that Bryan is spending more and more time with, even though he never actually broke up with your beloved Ten Minute Podcast. Along with Brendan Schaub , Bryan hosts The Fighter and The Kid and has failed to bring up TMP on the air the entire time their show has been on. Will is there to rectify that, hijacking their airtime to record his own ten minutes for you. Also, Will does a silly bit they like to do wherein he and the guys pretend to cut to a prerecorded interview with Hulk Hogan but its really just Will going in and out of the impersonation. Brendan attempts to take shots at the iconic Hulkster, which Will SIMPLY will NOT allow. And Bryan sorta kills the bit… because it is a bit... and that is what Bryan does to bits.
